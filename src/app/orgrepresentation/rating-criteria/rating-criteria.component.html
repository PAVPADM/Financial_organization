Rating Criteria for Organizations:

1) The Organization applied for rating must be a Financial Organization.
2) The Financial Organization applied for rating should be an Indian Located Company.
3) The Financial Organization applied for rating should hold minimum of 30crs Net Capital.
4) The Financial Organization applied for rating should have Certified BICRA Economic Risk Score.
5) The Financial Organization applied for rating should have Certified BICRA Industry Risk Score.
6) The Rating for the Financial Organization will be provided by the Finance Team by considering all the above received information.

What is BICRA ?

BICRA stands for “Banking Industry Country Risk Assessment”. It is a methodology which gives scores to the banking/financial organization based on their Economic Risk and Industry Risk.
